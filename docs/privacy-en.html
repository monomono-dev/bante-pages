Privacy Policy

BAN.TE — Golf Practice Support App

1. Data Handling

BAN.TE stores data such as practice logs, swing notes, and packing checklists in a local database on your device. No account registration or login is required for basic use.

2. Cloud Backup with Google Account

This app offers an optional feature to back up your data to the cloud by signing in with a Google account. When you use this feature, the following information is collected and stored:

  • Your Google account email address (used by Firebase Authentication for sign-in)
  • Practice logs, shot memos, swing notes, packing checklists, and settings (stored in Google Cloud Firestore via encrypted connection)

This data is accessible only by you and is never shared with or sold to third parties. If you do not use the backup feature, all data remains on your device and is never transmitted externally.

3. Analytics & Tracking

This app does not include any analytics tools or tracking SDKs. Your usage is not tracked in any way.

4. External Links

This app includes convenience links to Amazon.co.jp for gear search. Tapping a link will open your device's default browser.

Please refer to each site's own privacy policy for information on how they handle your data.

5. Data Deletion

Uninstalling the app from your device will delete all locally stored data.

If you have used the cloud backup feature, you can permanently delete your backup data by selecting "Delete Cloud Data" from the My Page screen within the app.
